# What I Learned About Stocks Today

## What is a Stock?

A stock represents ownership in a company. When a person buys a stock, they become a shareholder and own a small portion of that company.

Companies issue stocks to raise money for expansion, research, operations, and growth without taking loans.

Investors buy stocks as  they expect the company to grow in the future. As the company grows, the stock price may increase, allowing investors to earn profits.

## Stock Market Basics

The stock market is a place where buyers and sellers trade shares of publicly listed companies. In India, the major stock exchanges are NSE and BSE.

## Important Things I Learned

* A stock represents ownership.
* Shareholders own a part of a company.
* Stock prices change based on demand and supply.
* Investors can earn through capital appreciation and dividends.
* Understanding businesses is important before investing.
